One Minute
Your time limit is one minute only.  Within this time period you have type correct word as many as you can.
Two Minutes
Your time limit is two minutes. Within this time period you have to type a paragraph given to you. Your challenge is to type words correctly.
Three Minutes
Your time limit is three minutes. Within this time period you have to type a paragraph given to you. Your challenge is to type words correctly.
Arcade
In this game mode, you have to type the given word correctly to get the next word. Here, your challenge is to type all the words in the shortest possible time.
